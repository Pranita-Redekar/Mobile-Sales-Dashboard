<h1 align="center">📊 Mobile Sales Dashboard (Motorola Store)</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This project presents an interactive <b>Mobile Sales Dashboard</b> built using Power BI.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of mobile sales performance,
covering revenue, transactions, customer behavior, and product insights.
</p>

<p>
It helps in transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights for better
business decision-making.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Project Objectives</h2>
<ul>
<li>Analyze overall sales and revenue performance.</li>
<li>Track total quantity sold and transactions.</li>
<li>Understand city-wise sales distribution.</li>
<li>Identify top-performing mobile brands and models.</li>
<li>Analyze customer ratings and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Evaluate payment method trends.</li>
</ul>

<h2>📈 Dashboard Features</h2>

<h3>1. Sales by City</h3>
<p>
Map visualization showing total sales distribution across cities like Mumbai,
Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and more.
</p>

<h3>2. Monthly Sales Trend</h3>
<p>
Line chart displaying total quantity sold across months to identify trends and seasonality.
</p>

<h3>3. Customer Ratings</h3>
<p>
Bar chart showing distribution of customer ratings from 1 to 5.
</p>

<h3>4. Transactions by Payment Method</h3>
<p>
Pie chart showing usage of payment methods such as <b>UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, and Cash</b>.
</p>

<h3>5. Brand Performance</h3>
<p>
Table comparing brands like <b>Apple, Samsung, Vivo, OnePlus, and Xiaomi</b>
based on sales, quantity, and transactions.
</p>

<h3>6. Sales by Mobile Model</h3>
<p>
Bar chart highlighting top-selling models like <b>iPhone SE, OnePlus, Galaxy Series</b>.
</p>

<h3>7. Sales by Day</h3>
<p>
Line chart showing variation in sales across different days of the week.
</p>

<h2>📌 Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
<li>Major cities like Mumbai and Delhi contribute high sales.</li>
<li>Apple and Samsung are top-performing brands.</li>
<li>UPI is the most preferred payment method.</li>
<li>Sales fluctuate across months, with peak performance mid-year.</li>
<li>Customer ratings are mostly between 3 and 5.</li>
</ul>
